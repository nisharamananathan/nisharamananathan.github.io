Data Visualization Project CS 416

This dataset contains information about passengers on the Titanic, including an ID, whether a passenger survived or not, class (1, 2, or 3), name, sex, age, number of siblings or spouses on the Titanic, number of parents or children on the Titanic, ticket number, and ticket fare. The charts below dissect this information to determine 3 things:

(1) The percent of passengers that survived vs did not survive the Titanic. This chart also includes details on the gender distribution of men and women who survived vs did not survive.

(2) The amount of passengers in each class that did not survive the Titanic

(3) The percent of passengers on the Titanic who were men and women. This chart also provides an overview of the average age of these passengers on the Titanic.



Pie Chart of Men vs Women on the Titanic

This pie chart shows the percentage of men vs women passengers on the Titanic.



As shown, a higher percentage of passengers on the Titanic were men compared to women. Only 35.2% of passengers were women while 64.8% of passengers were male.
